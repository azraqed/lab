Welcome to the Psycholinguistics Lab

The Psycholinguistics Lab at [University Name] explores the cognitive and neural mechanisms underlying language processing. Our research focuses on how humans produce, understand, and acquire language, with particular interest in the interaction between linguistic and cognitive processes.

We use a variety of experimental methods including behavioral testing, neuroimaging, and computational modeling. Our work aims to uncover the cognitive systems that support language, as well as the ways in which these systems interact with other cognitive functions such as memory and attention.

The lab is committed to fostering an interdisciplinary environment for researchers interested in linguistics, psychology, and neuroscience. We welcome collaboration and encourage students and researchers from various disciplines to join us in advancing the understanding of language and the mind.
